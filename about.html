About Dr. Doria Mira

Dedicated to providing compassionate, patient-centered care

Meet Dr. Mira

Dr. Doria Mira is a compassionate and skilled family physician based at the Lotus Medical Clinic in Shawville, Quebec. With a strong commitment to improving patient outcomes, Dr. Mira provides personalized and comprehensive care for individuals and families across all stages of life.

She is part of a multidisciplinary team at the clinic, which is known for its modern facilities and patient-centric approach.

Her Journey

Dr. Mira’s training includes a Medical Doctorate from the Université de Sherbrooke and a residency in family medicine at the Université de Montréal. This foundation has equipped her with the expertise to address a wide range of health concerns while building lasting relationships with her patients.

Mission & Values

At the Lotus Medical Clinic, Dr. Mira contributes to the clinic’s mission of offering accessible, inclusive, and high-quality healthcare services to the Pontiac community. From routine checkups to urgent care, she is dedicated to meeting the unique health needs of her patients.

For more information or to schedule an appointment, visit the Lotus Medical Clinic.
